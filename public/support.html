Help Center

Find answers to common questions and get help with Amplifi.

Getting Started

How do I create an account?
Click the "Sign Up" button on the homepage and fill in your email, password, and username. You'll receive a confirmation email to verify your account.
How do I upload my first post?
After logging in, click the "+" button in the top navigation. You can upload photos, videos, or create text posts. Add a caption and hashtags to reach more people.
What types of content can I share?
You can share photos, videos, live streams, and text posts. We support most common image and video formats including JPG, PNG, MP4, and MOV.

Account & Profile

How do I change my profile picture?
Go to your profile page and click on your current profile picture. You can then upload a new image from your device or take a new photo.
Can I change my username?
Yes, you can change your username once every 30 days. Go to Settings > Profile and click "Edit Username". Note that your old username will become available for others to use.
How do I reset my password?
On the login page, click "Forgot Password?" and enter your email address. You'll receive a link to reset your password securely.

Content & Sharing

How do I go live?
Click the "+" button and select "Go Live". You can add a title and description for your stream. Make sure you have a stable internet connection for the best experience.
How do I use hashtags?
Add hashtags to your posts by typing # followed by your keyword (e.g., #food, #travel). Hashtags help others discover your content and can increase your reach.
Can I schedule posts?
Currently, we don't support post scheduling, but this feature is coming soon! For now, you can create drafts and post them when you're ready.

Privacy & Safety

How do I make my account private?
Go to Settings > Privacy and toggle "Private Account". With a private account, only approved followers can see your posts.
How do I block someone?
Go to their profile and click the three dots menu. Select "Block User". Blocked users cannot see your posts or contact you.
How do I report inappropriate content?
Click the three dots on any post and select "Report". Choose the reason for reporting and provide additional details if needed. We review all reports within 24 hours.

Monetization

How do I start earning money?
Once you have 1,000 followers, you can apply for the Creator Program. This allows you to receive tips from viewers and earn money through ads on your content.
How do tips work?
Viewers can send you tips during live streams or on your posts. Tips are processed securely and you'll receive 90% of the tip amount (10% goes to platform fees).
When do I get paid?
Payments are processed monthly. You need a minimum balance of $50 to receive a payout. Payments are made via PayPal or direct bank transfer.

Technical Support

The app is not loading properly
Try refreshing the page or clearing your browser cache. If the problem persists, check your internet connection or try using a different browser.
My upload is taking too long
Large files may take longer to upload. Try compressing your video or image, or check your internet connection speed. We recommend uploads under 100MB for faster processing.
I can't log in to my account
Make sure you're using the correct email and password. If you've forgotten your password, use the "Forgot Password?" link. If you're still having issues, contact our support team.
